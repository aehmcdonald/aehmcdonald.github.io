Healthy Hearth

Healthy Hearth Coffee The Logo The logo represents a home, with a hearth inside of it, as well as the hearth itself with a fire inside. The shape of the fire intentionally resembles a crown, to show the luxury that can be found in simple, rustic food and drink. Colors The colors are representative of the earth, the sky, and the coffee beans used by the cafe.
